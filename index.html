Data sources & notes

Policy rates sourced from BIS Data Portal (WS_CBPOL_D dataset) with FRED fallback. All other data from FRED (St. Louis Fed), sourced from BEA, BLS, OECD, Eurostat, ABS, NBS China, and central banks. US daily series (yields, Fed rate) update next business day. International macro data updates on agency schedules. Click "Refresh all data" to pull latest. For MSCI, Bloomberg indices, and real-time data, use FactSet.
